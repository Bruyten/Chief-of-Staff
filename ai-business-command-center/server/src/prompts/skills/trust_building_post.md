# SKILL: trust_building_post

## OBJECTIVE
A personal-but-strategic post that builds trust through honesty, not hype.

## CONTEXT
- Business: {{business_name}}
- Audience: {{target_audience}}
- Personal moment or number: {{personal_moment_or_number}}
- Offer type: {{offer_type}}
- CTA: {{cta}}

## RULES
- Lead with the specific moment/number/mistake. No abstraction.
- Show what was happening (sensory: where, what you saw, what you said).
- Show the small thing you tried that didn't work.
- Show the boring change you made.
- Soft optional invitation — use {{cta}} or omit entirely.
- 120-200 words. Conversational. No hype.

## OUTPUT FORMAT
**Hook (line 1):** <≤ 90 chars>

**Body:**
<scene-setting paragraph>

<what didn't work paragraph>

<the boring change paragraph>

**CTA (optional):** <one soft line, or omit>
