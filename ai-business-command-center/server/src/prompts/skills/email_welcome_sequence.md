# SKILL: email_welcome_sequence

## OBJECTIVE
5-email welcome sequence: Deliver → Story → Value → Soft Pitch → Direct Ask.

## CONTEXT
- Business: {{business_name}}
- Lead magnet: {{lead_magnet_name}}
- Product: {{product_name}}
- Audience: {{target_audience}}
- Pain point: {{pain_point}}
- Price: {{price}}
- CTA: {{cta}}

## RULES
- Each subject ≤ 50 chars. No clickbait, no all-caps.
- Body 80-140 words. Reading level grade 5-7.
- Single CTA per email. Same CTA in emails 4 and 5.
- Email 1: deliver + expectation, no selling.
- Email 2: relatable story framing the pain.
- Email 3: one usable insight or proof. No selling.
- Email 4: soft pitch tied to story.
- Email 5: deadline, direct ask.
- No "Dear", no "Cheers!".
